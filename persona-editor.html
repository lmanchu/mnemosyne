How this works

This is your persona file — a markdown document that describes who you are to the AI.

AI-generated sections are synthesized from your screen activity (ActivityCards). They update automatically as you use your computer.

Your edits take priority. If you change something, the AI won't overwrite it. Add social profiles, interests, and personal notes to enrich your persona.

Social Profiles: Paste URLs/handles. Mnemosyne can scan them to understand your professional identity and interests.

Entertainment & Culture: Your favorites help the AI use references and analogies you'll appreciate.

My Notes: Anything else the AI should know — communication preferences, pet peeves, goals.
